UX Design Process

Before developing the final website, I followed a structured UX process using Figma to create a one-page, seamless scrolling experience that ensures an intuitive user journey.

  • Research and Planning

    - Developed a logical structure and intuitive navigation for a smooth user journey.

    - Focused on visual storytelling with a strong emphasis on product imagery and customer engagement.

    - Ensured easy accessibility of key content and interactions.

  • Mood Board and Style Guide

    - Designed a mood board to define the brand’s natural, earthy, and creative identity.

    - Selected a color palette inspired by nature, with green, soft neutrals, and purple accents for a vibrant and fresh feel.

    - Chose typography that reflects handcrafted aesthetics with a modern touch, aligning with the brand’s artisanal approach.

    - Incorporated organic textures and natural elements into the visual style.

  • Wireframing and Prototyping

    - Designed a wireframe to ensure a clean and structured scrolling layout.

    - Prioritized visual storytelling with large product images and interactive elements to enhance engagement.

    - Ensured smooth social media integration, making it easy for users to explore and interact with DIG’s community.
