UX Design Process

Before developing the final website, I followed a structured UX process using Figma to ensure a well-organized, intuitive, and visually calming user experience. This approach focused on creating a clean and peaceful design that aligns with the wellness and mindfulness industry.

  • Research and Planning

    - Conducted research on meditation and wellness websites to identify the best UX/UI practices.

    - Designed a site map to create a logical structure for smooth and intuitive navigation.

    - Prioritized accessibility and usability, ensuring that key sections were easy to locate.

  • Mood Board and Style Guide

    - Created a mood board in Figma to capture the brand’s serene and harmonious aesthetic.

    - Selected a soft, neutral color palette inspired by nature, using calming blues and earthy tones.

    - Chose typography that balances modern elegance and readability, enhancing the peaceful atmosphere.

  • Wireframing and Prototyping

    - Designed wireframes for desktop views, ensuring a structured and readable layout.

    - Focused on a minimalist, content-driven design that highlights essential information without distractions.

    - Prioritized readability and intuitive navigation, making it easy for users to find meditation schedules and center information.
