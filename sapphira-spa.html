UX Design Process

Before developing the final website for Sapphira Spa Salon, I followed a structured UX process using Figma to conduct research, establish the brand identity, and refine the user flow. The goal was to create an intuitive, luxurious, and visually soothing digital experience that reflects the spa’s elegance and high-end services.

  • Research and Planning

    - Conducted market research on high-end spa websites to understand user expectations for luxury wellness brands.

    - Designed a site map to ensure seamless navigation across various service categories, from treatments to online reservations and an integrated e-commerce store.

    - Focused on usability and accessibility to provide a smooth browsing experience for all users.

  • Mood Board and Style Guide

    - Established a luxurious and tranquil brand identity with a gold and deep blue color palette - elegance and trust.

    - Selected high-quality typography that blends sophistication with readability.

    - Curated brand imagery emphasizing relaxation, self-care, and an exclusive spa experience.

  • Wireframing and Prototyping

    - Created wireframes for both desktop and mobile-first design, ensuring an intuitive user experience.

    - Designed a structured layout that emphasizes the spa’s expertise and service highlights.

    - Prioritized online reservations, making it easy for users to book appointments.
