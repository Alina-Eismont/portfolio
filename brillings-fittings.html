UX Design Process

Before designing the final website, I followed a structured UX process using Figma for research, planning, and layout development. This approach helped ensure a seamless user experience while maintaining a professional and industrial aesthetic.

  • Research and Planning

    - Conducted market research on competitor websites to analyze best practices in industrial and manufacturing website design.

    - Designed a site map to define the website’s navigation structure and ensure an intuitive user flow.

    - Focused on accessibility and usability, considering the primary audience of engineers, manufacturers, and business clients.

  • Mood Board and Style Guide

    - Developed a moodboard to establish the visual direction of the site, ensuring consistency in branding.

    - Selected a color palette that reflects the precision and professionalism of the industry.

    - Chose typography that conveys a modern and technical feel while maintaining readability.

  • Wireframing and Prototyping

    - Created wireframes for desktop and mobile views, ensuring a clean, structured layout.

    - Prioritized product information and company credibility, placing key details above the fold.

    - Focused on a responsive and scalable design, ensuring usability across all devices.
