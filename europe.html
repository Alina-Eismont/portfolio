UX Design Process

Before developing the final website, I followed a structured UX process using Figma to create a visually appealing and informative platform that enhances user experience and provides an engaging interface for travel enthusiasts.

  • Research and Planning

    - Developed a site map to structure navigation efficiently, ensuring an intuitive browsing experience.

    - To attract users, focus on highlighting destination information, images, and engagement options.

    - Focused on accessibility, ensuring key travel information was easy to locate.

  • Mood Board and Style Guide

    - Designed a mood board to establish the brand’s adventurous, cultural, and scenic aesthetic.

    - Chose a vibrant yet calming color palette inspired by travel and exploration.

    - Selected typography that balances elegance and modernity, making travel details easy to read.

  • Wireframing and Prototyping

    - Created wireframes to structure key content sections clearly and logically.

    - Designed with a horizontal stripe layout to improve readability and segmentation of content.

    - Focused on destination highlights, ensuring users can quickly find relevant travel information.
