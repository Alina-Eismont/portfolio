UX Design Process

Before developing the final website, I followed a structured UX process using Figma to conduct research, plan the layout, and define the visual identity. This process involved creating a site map, mood board, and wireframes to ensure a well-organized, user-friendly experience before moving into development.

  • Research and Planning

    - Conducted market research on competitor websites and user expectations to understand industry trends and audience needs.

    - Designed a site map to structure navigation logically, ensuring a smooth and intuitive user flow.

    - Focused on usability and accessibility, making sure key content was easy to find.

  • Mood Board and Style Guide

    - Established the brand’s visual identity (a warm, inviting, and bookish aesthetic.)

    - Defined a cozy, earthy color palette to reflect the coffeehouse ambiance.

    - Selected typography that balances modern readability with artisanal charm.

  • Wireframing and Prototyping

    - Created wireframes for desktop views, focusing on a clean, content-driven layout.

    - Ensured scalability for responsive design, allowing seamless adaptation across devices.
